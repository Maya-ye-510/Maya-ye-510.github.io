Cruella


Director: Craig Gillespie

Cruella (2021) is a prequel to 101 Dalmatians that explores the backstory of the iconic villain, Cruella de Vil. The film is set in 1970s London and follows Estella, a young woman with a talent for fashion design, who is determined to make a name for herself. After a series of events, Estella adopts the persona of "Cruella," a rebellious, edgy figure with a flair for extravagant fashion. The film delves into her rivalry with the wealthy fashion mogul, the Baroness, and showcases the transformation of Estella into the infamous villain while exploring themes of identity, revenge, and ambition.
