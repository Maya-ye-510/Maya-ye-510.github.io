Minions

Directors: Pierre Coffin and Kyle Balda

"Minions" is a 2015 animated film that serves as a prequel to the "Despicable Me" series. It follows the quirky, yellow creatures known as Minions, who have served various villains throughout history. After losing their last master, the Minions embark on a journey to find a new one. They encounter a female supervillain named Scarlet Overkill, who has grand plans for world domination. The film showcases their comedic adventures and mishaps as they try to impress Scarlet while staying true to their goofy nature. Ultimately, it's a fun, family-friendly story about friendship and loyalty.
