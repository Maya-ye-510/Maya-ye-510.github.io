Spider-man: Across
the Spider-Verse

Directors: Joaquim Santos, Kemp Powers, & Justin Thompson

Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse (2023) is an animated superhero film and the sequel to Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse (2018). The story follows Miles Morales, the young Spider-Man, as he is once again thrust into the multiverse, where he encounters different versions of Spider-People from alternate dimensions. Miles teams up with Gwen Stacy (Spider-Woman) and confronts new challenges as a mysterious group, led by the formidable Spider-Man 2099 (Miguel O'Hara), has a plan that could threaten the stability of the multiverse. As Miles grapples with his identity and his role in the larger Spider-Verse, the film explores themes of fate, choice, and responsibility, while delivering a visually stunning and action-packed experience.
