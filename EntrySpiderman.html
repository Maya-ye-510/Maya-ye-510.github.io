Spider-man: Across
the Spider-Verse

Directors: Joaquim Santos, Kemp Powers, & Justin Thompson

"Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse" is the 2023 sequel to "Into the Spider-Verse." The film follows Miles Morales as he continues to embrace his role as Spider-Man. He reunites with Gwen Stacy and embarks on a multiverse adventure, encountering various Spider-People from different dimensions. As they confront a powerful new villain, Miles grapples with his identity and the responsibilities that come with being a hero. The film explores themes of friendship, sacrifice, and the complexities of multiple realities, all while delivering stunning animation and a captivating story.
